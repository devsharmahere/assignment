Sano Manjiro (Mikey)

The Invincible Mikey - Tokyo Revengers

Background

Sano Manjiro, also known as Mikey, is a central character in the anime and manga series "Tokyo Revengers." Known as "The Invincible Mikey," he is the fearless leader of the Tokyo Manji Gang. With a complex personality, he balances a fierce loyalty to his friends with a mysterious, often dark side.
